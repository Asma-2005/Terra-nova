Our Mission

Terra-Nova is revolutionizing how cities manage food waste and distribution. We combine AI-powered food analysis with NASA Earth observation data to create sustainable food systems that benefit communities, reduce environmental impact, and support urban planning.

Our platform connects food donors with organizations in need while converting food waste into valuable resources for urban agriculture. Through data-driven insights and community collaboration, we're building smarter, more sustainable cities.
