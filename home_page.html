How It Works

1
🤖

AI Food Rescue

Our advanced AI analyzes food images and classifies them as Fresh, At-Risk, or Spoiled, ensuring safe and efficient food redistribution.

2
♻️

Waste-to-Resource

Spoiled food is automatically routed to composting facilities or urban gardens, creating nutrient-rich soil for sustainable agriculture.

3
📈

Urban Planning Dashboard

Planners leverage NASA Earth observation data to make informed decisions about food systems, green infrastructure, and resource allocation.
