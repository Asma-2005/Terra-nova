Environmental Impact Summary

Multi-Source Validation

Methane Reduction

By diverting 92.4% of organic waste from landfills to composting facilities, the system prevents approximately 847 tons of CO₂-equivalent methane emissions monthly.

Urban Green Space

HLS data identifies 12 suitable sites for urban farming, totaling 94,500 m² of available land for sustainable food production and community gardens.

Soil Quality Improvement

NDVI monitoring shows average vegetation index increase of 0.23 points across urban farms using Terra-Nova compost over 6 months.
